# Masterplan

## 1. Vision
Build a sophisticated "CFO for STR" platform that helps property managers optimize their operations through data.

## 2. Strategic Phases

### Phase 1: Foundation (Architecture & Auth)
- Robust DB schema with RLS.
- Astro Middleware for route protection.

### Phase 2: Ingestion Engine (The ETL)
- Bulletproof CSV processor.
- Mapping UI for unmapped listings.

### Phase 3: Operational Control (OPEX)
- Expense tracking system.
- Accounts payable management.

### Phase 4: Financial Intelligence (Dashboard)
- Advanced KPI calculation engine.
- Interactive visualizations.

### Phase 5: Reporting & Automation
- PDF/Excel export.
- Alerts for break-even and maintenance.
